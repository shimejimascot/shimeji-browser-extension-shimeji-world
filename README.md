# Shimeji World: The Ultimate Browser Mascot Experience 🌟

**Shimeji World** is the premier browser extension designed to transform your digital workspace into an interactive playground. We bring high-quality, animated mascots directly to your screen, creating a unique browsing experience for fans across all major fandoms.

## ✨ Beyond Just Anime

While many know us for our anime companions, **Shimeji World** is a comprehensive hub for diverse interests. Our library is meticulously curated to include:

*   **Global Icons**: From **K-pop idols** and **Celebrities** to **Sports legends**.
*   **Pop Culture**: Extensive collections of **Cartoons**, **Comics**, and **Video Games**.
*   **Custom Creators**: A growing space for unique, original **others** and community mascots.

## 🚀 Why Choose Shimeji World?

*   **Fully Interactive**: Our characters don't just sit on your screen—they crawl, dance, sit, and interact with the websites you visit in real-time.
*   **Seamless Integration**: Optimized for **Chrome**, **Edge**, and other Chromium browsers to ensure smooth performance without slowing down your computer.
*   **Weekly Content Drops**: We update our library every week with new animations and characters from trending series and groups.
*   **Cross-Platform Ecosystem**: Beyond the browser, explore our productivity tools like **Shimpo** (Pomodoro Timer) and our physical merchandise at the [**Shimpo app**](https://shimpo.aquariusapp.xyz).
